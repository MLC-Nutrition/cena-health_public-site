# Brand Voice Rules - CenaHealth

## Source of Truth

**All brand voice guidelines are maintained in [voice.md](voice.md).**

That file contains:
- Core voice principles (direct/concise, accuracy over speed, healthcare infrastructure focus)
- Writing style guidelines and tone variations by audience
- Language preferences and words to avoid
- Brand-specific requirements and messaging framework
- Technical communication style
- Content creation guidelines for investors, documentation, and marketing
- Visual style references and brand assets
- Quality checklist and common corrections

## Apply Voice Project-Wide

The voice defined in [voice.md](voice.md) applies to **all text content and documents** in this project:
- Website copy and content
- Investor materials and presentations
- Technical documentation
- Marketing content
- Sitemaps and planning documents
- Research reports and analysis
- Strategy documents
- Email communications
- Social media content
- Any written deliverable

## Brand-Specific Context (for this directory)

When working in `/brand/`:
- This directory contains brand assets, voice guidelines, and visual identity
- [voice.md](voice.md) is the authoritative voice guide
- Future additions may include: logo files, color palettes, typography samples, brand guidelines PDF

**For all voice and tone questions, reference [voice.md](voice.md).**
