## Shared Components Across All Sites

### Common Elements
- Responsive Design
- Accessibility Features (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Multi-language Support (English/Spanish)
- HIPAA Compliance (where applicable)
- Secure Authentication
- Analytics & Tracking

### Cross-Site Integration Points
- Unified Brand Identity
- Shared Blog/Resource Content (filtered by audience)
- Centralized CMS
- Single Sign-On (SSO) for authenticated users
- Unified Contact/Support System

### Technical Considerations
- Subdomain Structure Options:
  - patients.domain.com
  - providers.domain.com
  - partners.domain.com
- OR Path-based Structure:
  - domain.com (patients)
  - domain.com/providers
  - domain.com/partners

## Content Strategy Notes

### Patient Site Focus
- Simple, accessible language
- Empathy and support-focused
- Clear eligibility information
- Condition-specific content
- Visual meal examples
- Trust signals (testimonials, certifications)

### Provider Site Focus
- Clinical evidence and outcomes
- ROI and cost-effectiveness
- Integration workflows
- Professional development
- Peer-reviewed research
- Accreditations and quality metrics

### Partner Site Focus
- Business opportunity and scale
- Market position and growth
- Partnership benefits and requirements
- Innovation and technology
- Social impact metrics
- Investment opportunities
