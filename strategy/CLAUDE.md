# Strategy Directory Rules

## Purpose of This Directory

The `/strategy/` directory contains audience definitions, messaging frameworks, go-to-market planning, and strategic positioning. This is where research insights become actionable plans.

## Source of Truth Documents

### [target-audiences.md](target-audiences.md) - Audience Definitions & Messaging

**This is the authoritative source for:**
- Primary audience segments (3 health system maturity levels)
- Secondary audiences (CBOs, payers, investors)
- Pain points and core needs by segment
- Key messages tailored to each audience
- Language to use for each segment
- Portal flow by audience maturity

**When creating content for any audience:**
1. Check [target-audiences.md](target-audiences.md) for their segment definition first
2. Understand their maturity level / current state
3. Match messaging to their core need
4. Use their language and frame benefits in their terms
5. Address their specific objections

## Strategy Content in This Directory

Files in `/strategy/` should focus on:
- **Audience segmentation** and persona development
- **Messaging frameworks** by audience and funnel stage
- **Go-to-market strategy** and market entry planning
- **Positioning** and category creation
- **Objection handling** and sales enablement
- **Content strategy** by audience type

## Working with Strategy Documents

### When Developing Messaging

1. **Start with audience:** Which segment from [target-audiences.md](target-audiences.md)?
2. **Identify their stage:** Awareness, consideration, or decision?
3. **Match pain to solution:** What specific problem are we solving for them?
4. **Apply voice:** Use guidelines from [brand/voice.md](../brand/voice.md)
5. **Support with proof:** Pull metrics from research/core-competencies or sitemaps
6. **Include CTA:** Every message should move toward action

### When Creating Positioning

**Category we own:**
"Healthcare Infrastructure for Prescription-Based Nutrition Care"

**Not:** Meal delivery, care coordination platform, food vendor
**We are:** Complete nutrition-first care delivery platform with VBC enablement

**Competitive positioning:**
- vs. Meal delivery: We deliver clinical outcomes, not just food
- vs. Care platforms: We deliver comprehensive care with food component
- vs. Hospital programs: We're turnkey with proven ROI, not experimental

## Quality Standards

Before finalizing any strategy document:

- [ ] Tied to specific audience segment(s) from [target-audiences.md](target-audiences.md)
- [ ] Includes proof points with data sources
- [ ] Addresses known objections
- [ ] Clear differentiation from competitors
- [ ] Actionable (what should we DO with this?)
- [ ] Consistent with brand voice from [brand/voice.md](../brand/voice.md)
- [ ] Updated with latest data/metrics

## Strategic Questions to Always Ask

1. **Who is this for?** Which specific audience segment?
2. **What do they care about?** Pain point or goal?
3. **Why us?** Which competency/differentiator matters most?
4. **Why now?** What creates urgency?
5. **What proof?** What evidence supports this claim?
6. **What action?** What do we want them to do?
7. **What objection?** What might make them hesitate?
8. **How do we win?** Against competitors and status quo?
